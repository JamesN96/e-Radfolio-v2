Domain 4: radiation safety and risk management

The importance of radiation safety is something that is drilled in to all students when they first start this course. The importance of achieving the ALARA principle. During my time at placements, we must demonstrate to our supervisors that we know what exposure factors are required for different types of patients. Knowing when to go up or down . That for paediatrics we must minimise the amount of images we need and ensuring that parents who accompany their children during the x-rays must be given appropriate lead shielding. During theatre as well, before exposing we must make sure that everyone in the room is either covered or protected behind a glass.
